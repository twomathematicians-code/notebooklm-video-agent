[Project] I built an AI tool that turns audio + PowerPoint into YouTube videos

Hey everyone!

I created an open-source Python tool that automatically converts 
audio files (from NotebookLM, podcasts, or recordings) into 
professional YouTube videos by syncing them with PowerPoint slides.

**Why I built it:**
I love NotebookLM's AI podcasts, but creating videos from them 
took hours. Now it takes 5 minutes.

**How it works:**
1. Upload your audio file
2. Add your slide images
3. AI syncs them together
4. Generates captions automatically
5. Outputs YouTube-ready video

**GitHub:** https://github.com/twomathematicians-code/podcast-video-creation-from-ppt-and-audio
